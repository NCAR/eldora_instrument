


Directions to Sandia
--------------------

Go directly to area 4 -- about 1 mi. south of building 800.


Take Gibson Blvd. to Wyoming Blvd. Turn Right on Wyoming Blvd. 
Head past B-52 and go left on Harden Blvd. (traffic light). Take 
a right at the 4-way stop. Head south about 1/2 mi. There should
be a brown 4-story building on the left (#962). Call Al from guard
gate (844-6866)
